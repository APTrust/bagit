DART is the Digital (Asset/Archivist's) Routing Tool for packaging and sending data. It produces BagIt files and uploads them to any S3-compliant API.

This is open source software produced and maintained by the Academic Preservation Trust on GitHub. DART's plugin architecture allows developers to add packaging formats (tar, rar, zip, etc.) and network protocols (ftp, sftp, etc.).
